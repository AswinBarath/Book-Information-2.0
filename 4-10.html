    • Description

    Bitcoin is starting to come into its own as a digital currency, but the blockchain technology behind it
    could prove to be much more significant. This book takes you beyond the currency ("Blockchain 1.0") and
    smart contracts ("Blockchain 2.0") to demonstrate how the blockchain is in position to become the fifth
    disruptive computing paradigm after mainframes, PCs, the Internet, and mobile/social networking.
